Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation drives progress by introducing new ideas and technological advancements

Sustainability

Sustainability ensures long-term environmental health and economic resilience.

Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure enhances connectivity, attracts investments, and boosts economic growth.

Empowerment

Empowerment creates opportunities for individuals to contribute and thrive inclusively.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Innovation is not just about having a brilliant new idea; it's about the successful implementation of that idea within a community. The process involves understanding the needs of the community, effectively communicating the vision, and fostering an environment where the idea can be adopted and sustained. True innovation requires persistence, collaboration, and a willingness to adapt. It's not the solitary genius but the collective effort that brings transformative ideas to life and integrates them into the fabric of society."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
